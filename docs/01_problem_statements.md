# Problem Statement

## Background

Traffic congestion is one of the biggest challenges in modern cities. Most traffic signals operate on fixed time intervals without considering the actual number of vehicles waiting at an intersection.

As a result, vehicles often wait unnecessarily even when other roads have very little traffic.

---

## Existing Problems

- Fixed-time traffic signals
- Long waiting time
- Heavy traffic congestion
- Fuel wastage
- Increased air pollution
- Delays for emergency vehicles
- No real-time traffic analysis

---

## Proposed Solution

The Smart Traffic Management System aims to solve these problems by using Computer Vision and Artificial Intelligence to detect vehicle density in real time.

Based on the detected traffic, the system can optimize signal timings automatically, helping improve traffic flow and reduce unnecessary waiting.

---

## Expected Benefits

- Better traffic management
- Reduced congestion
- Less fuel consumption
- Reduced pollution
- Faster emergency response
- Smart city integration
